# Reg P Privacy Disclosures — Annual Notice and Revised Notice Triggers (12 CFR 1016.5(a), 1016.8)

**Source:** bulletin_2013_9a.pdf
**Chunk ID:** bulletin_2013_9a_001
**Section Path:** Reg P Privacy Disclosures — Annual Notice and Revised Notice Triggers (12 CFR 1016.5(a), 1016.8)
**Summary:** Defines the annual privacy notice requirement for national banks and Federal savings associations and the obligation to provide a revised privacy notice (and new opt-out notice) when information-sharing practices change in a way inconsistent with prior notices. Key entities include institution types (national bank, Federal savings association), customers/consumers, and privacy policy disclosures.

---

§ 1016.5(a)—Disclosure (institution)— 
Annual privacy notice to customers requirement —A national bank or 
Federal savings association must provide a clear and conspicuous notice to customers that accurately reflects its privacy policies and practices not less than annually during the continuation of the customer relationship. 
§ 1016.8—Disclosure (institution)— 
Revised privacy notices —If a national 
bank or Federal savings association wishes to disclose information in a way that is inconsistent with the notices previously given to a consumer, the national bank or Federal savings association must provide consumers with a clear and conspicuous revised notice of the national bank’s or Federal savings association’s policies and procedures and a new opt out notice.
