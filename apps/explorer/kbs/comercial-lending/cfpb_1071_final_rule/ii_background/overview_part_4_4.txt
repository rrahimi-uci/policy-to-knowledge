The precise impacts of these broader trends are not well understood at present because 
there are no comprehensive, comparable, and application -level data across the fragmented and 
complex small business lending market. Some small business lending data exist, provided in data 
reported to Federal regulators, but available data are incomplete in certain ways. Some do not 
include lending by certain categories of institutions, such as smaller depository institutions. And 
none include lending by nondepository institutions, which com prises almost half of all small 
business financing.32  
The datasets that do exist both over - and underestimate small business lending in certain 
respects by including small dollar loans to non-small businesses and by excluding larger loans to small busines ses.
33 Further, these datasets almost exclusively concern originated loans; they do
