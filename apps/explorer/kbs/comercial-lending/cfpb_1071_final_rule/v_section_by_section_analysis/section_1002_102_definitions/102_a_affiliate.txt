102(a) Affiliate  
Proposed Rule  
The Bureau proposed §  1002.102(a) to define “ affiliate” based on whether the term is 
used to refer to a financial institution or to an applicant.  
Proposed § 1002.102(a) would have defined “ affiliate” with respect to a financial 
institution as any company that controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with, 
another company, as set forth in the Bank Holding Company Act of 1956.
