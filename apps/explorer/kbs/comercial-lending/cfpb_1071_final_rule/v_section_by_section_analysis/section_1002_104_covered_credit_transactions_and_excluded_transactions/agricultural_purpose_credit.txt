Background

United States. The U.S. Departme nt of Agriculture (USDA) Economic Research Service found

90 percent of all U.S. farms and large-scale family farms ($1 million or more in gross cash farm 
income ) make up about 3 percent of farms but 44 percent of the value of production.393
