ii. Multi -step partial change s of ownership  are not eligible. A  multi -step partial 
change of ownership occurs when the change of ownership is structured such 
that some or all of the existing owners are bringing on a new owner(s) via the 
formation of a new entity that will become the 100% owner of the Operating 
Company, and where some or all of the existing owners and the new 
owner(s) will be the owners of the new entity.  
iii. 7(a) loans may not fund a partial change of ownership in an EPC for a 
business structured as an EPC/OC due to the regulation at 13 CFR § 120.111 . 
However, 7(a) loans may be used to fund a  partial change of ownership in the 
Operating Company of a business structured as an EPC/OC .
