D. 401(K) PLANS INCLUDING ROLLOVERS AS BUSINESS START -UPS (ROBS) 
PLANS  
A business that is owned in whole or in part by a 401(k) plan  (including a Rollovers as Business 
Startups (ROBS) plan)  may be eligible provided the SBA Lender complies with  the requirements 
in this section  and the plan complies with all applicable IRS, Treasury, and Department of Labor 
requirements.   
When evaluating  applications involving such businesses, SBA Lenders must consider that a  
401(k) plan sponsor’s failure to administer the plan properly may result in plan disqualification 
and adverse tax consequences to the plan’s sponsor and its participants, which may i mpact the 
Borrower’s ability to repay the loan.  
The SBA Lender must  confirm that  the ROBS Plan meets the requirements and conditions 
prescribed by other regulatory bodies . SBA will not review any applications, even those 
submitted via non -delegated processing, for compliance with the requirements imposed by other 
regulatory bodies  for these types of transactions.
