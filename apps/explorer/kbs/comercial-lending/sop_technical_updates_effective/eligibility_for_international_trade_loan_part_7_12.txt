3. Loan Terms and Conditions for International Trade  
a. Maximum Loan Amount  
i. The maximum loan amount is $5,000,000.  
ii. Maximum Loans to Businesses with Affiliates  
13 CFR § 120.151  Lenders must determine whether the Applicant has any 
affiliates  and document the results in their credit analysis. If affiliation exists, 
SBA’s loan maximums apply to the Applicant , including all affiliates, as if 
all were a single business.
