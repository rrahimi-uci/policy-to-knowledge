B3-4.2-04, Pooled Savings (Community Savings Funds) (04/01/2009)
Introduction
This topic contains information on pooled savings (community savings funds).
Pooled Savings (Community Savings Funds)
Pooled Savings (Community Savings Funds)
Funds from a community savings account or any other type of pooled savings may be used for the down
payment if the borrower can document regular contributions to the fund.
Acceptable documentation includes written conﬁrmation from the party managing the pooled savings fund and
documentation of regular borrower contributions.
The borrower’s obligation to continue making contributions to the fund must be considered as part of the
borrower’s debt when calculating the total debt-to-income ratio.
