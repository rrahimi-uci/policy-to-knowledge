Introduction
This topic contains information on the number and age of accounts.
Number and Age of Accounts
Number and Age of Accounts
The lender must review the borrower’s credit report to determine whether they have an older established credit
history or a newly established credit history, and whether there are a signiﬁcant number of recently opened
accounts or a mix of new accounts and older accounts.
Credit histories that include older, established accounts generally represent lower credit risk. However, an older,
established credit history that includes a signiﬁcant number of recently opened accounts may indicate that the
borrower is overextended, and thus will represent a higher credit risk.
