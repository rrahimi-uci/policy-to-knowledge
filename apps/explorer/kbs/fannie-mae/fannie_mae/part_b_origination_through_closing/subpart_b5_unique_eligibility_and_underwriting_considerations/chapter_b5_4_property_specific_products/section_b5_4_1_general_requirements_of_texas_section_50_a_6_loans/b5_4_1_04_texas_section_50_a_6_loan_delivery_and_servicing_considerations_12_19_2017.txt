756Title Insurance
For all Texas Section 50(a)(6) loans, a title insurance policy written on Texas Land Title Association forms
(standard or short form), supplemented by an Equity Loan Mortgage Endorsement (Form T-42) and a
Supplemental Coverage Equity Loan Mortgage Endorsement  (Form T-42.1), is required.
Note : There may be no exceptions or deletions to the coverage provided by Paragraphs 2(a) through
(e) (other than an exception or deletion relating to the exclusion of agricultural homestead property) of
the T-42 endorsement. The endorsement must include the optional coverage provided by Paragraph
2(f), as well as the additional coverage provided by Endorsement T-42.1.
The title insurance policy cannot include language that:
excludes coverage for a title defect that arises because ﬁnanced origination expenses are held not to be
“reasonable costs necessary to reﬁnance”, or
deﬁnes the “reasonable costs necessary to reﬁnance” requirement as a “consumer credit protection” law
since the standard title policy excludes coverage when lien validity is questioned due to a failure to
comply with consumer credit protection laws.
