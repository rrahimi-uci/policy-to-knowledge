Chapter C1-3, Loan Remittance Types Overview
Introduction
When selling loans to Fannie Mae, lenders must decide if they will service the loans or release the servicing to
another Fannie Mae-approved lender. They must also select a method for remitting borrower payments to
Fannie Mae. This chapter provides an overview regarding remitting payments to Fannie Mae.
