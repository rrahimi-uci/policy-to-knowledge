1033Part D, Ensuring Quality Control (QC)
Introduction
This part discusses the quality control process for lenders and Fannie Mae.
D1, Lender QC Process
This subpart describes Fannie Mae’s requirements for lender quality control programs and practices.
D2, Fannie Mae QC Process
This subpart describes the process Fannie Mae uses to ensure that the mortgage loans it purchases or
securitizes meet its eligibility and underwriting requirements.
