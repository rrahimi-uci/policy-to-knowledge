1172E-3-23, Acronyms and Glossary of Deﬁned Terms: W (11/10/2019)
weighted-average pool accrual rate
The weighted average of the net mortgage interest rates of the mortgages in a weighted-average ARM MBS
pool, which is the rate at which interest will accrue on the MBS.
weighted-average structure pooling
A method of creating an ARM MBS pool that results in interest accruals to the security holder at the weighted
average of the accrual rates of the mortgages in the pool.
whole loan delivery
The submission of a whole mortgage or a participation pool mortgage to Fannie Mae for purchase as a portfolio
mortgage. Fannie Mae pays the mortgage seller cash for its mortgage delivery, rather than swapping the
mortgage for a mortgage-backed security.
