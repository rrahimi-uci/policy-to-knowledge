10.9 Adverse circumstances – Mortgage (09/08/04)  
There must be no circumstances or conditions of which the Seller is aware involving the Mortgage 
that adversely affect the value or marketability of the Mortgage.   
 
10.10  Originator and origination fees (0 9/28/18) 
The Seller is fully liable for all warranties and representations made to Freddie Mac regardless of 
who originated the Mortgage, including the warranties and representations regarding origination 
fees in Section 5.3(d).
