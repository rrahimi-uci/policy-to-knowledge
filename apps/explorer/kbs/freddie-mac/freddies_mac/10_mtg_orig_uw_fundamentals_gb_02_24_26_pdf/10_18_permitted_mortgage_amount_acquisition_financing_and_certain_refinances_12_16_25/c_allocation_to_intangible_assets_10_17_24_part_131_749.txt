 Single Counsel for TEL transactions ( 08/17 /23) 
 Engagement of Single Counsel  (08/17 /23) 
 
Freddie Mac engages Single Counsel to jointly represent Freddie Mac and the Seller in 
connection with the TEL transaction.  Such representation may include the following services:  
 
• Review of and advice regarding any existing loan/bond documents  
 
• Advice concerning structure of the proposed transaction  
 
• Assistance in negotiations with the other parties to the proposed transaction  
 
• Participation in telephone calls related to the proposed transaction  
 
• Prepar ation of  (i) the Legal Issues Analysis, together with any other required legal analyses 
and (ii) the analysis of exceptions to title insurance coverage, all in accordance with the 
requirements of this Guide  
 
• Preparation of documents related to the proposed transaction  
 
• Review of documents prepared by other parties , including the funding loan agreement, tax-
exempt g overnmental note, project loan agreement, tax regulatory agreement and any other 
documents pertaining to the governmental lender , each of which will be prepared by bond 
counsel  
 
• If required by Freddie Mac, attendance at the closing of the proposed transaction as Freddie 
Mac’s and Seller ’s representative
