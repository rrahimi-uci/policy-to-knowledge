The Borrower’s TIN is required to complete Forms 1099 -A and 1099 -C.  If the Servicer does not 
have the Borrower’s TIN, the Servicer must make a reasonable effort to obtain the TIN.  Such 
request must clearly notify the Borrower that the IRS requires the Borrower to provide its TIN 
and that failure to do so subjects the Borrower to a $50 penalty imposed by the IRS.  To request 
the TIN, the Servicer must use IRS Form W -9, Request for Ta xpayer Identification Number and 
Certification.  However, the Borrower is not required to certify the TIN under penalties of perjury.
