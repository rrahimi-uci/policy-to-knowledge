Guide Chapter 62SBL – SBL Physical Risk Report Requirements  
62SBL.7  Storage tanks ( 02/16/23 ) 
Based on a property inspection and input sought from knowledgeable property sources, the 
physical risk consultant must complete a transaction screen questionnaire substantially similar 
to the current ASTM E1528 standard to initially evaluate this risk.  If  any responses to the 
questionnaire are “unknown”, the consultant must explain why this data gap is not a problem or 
an environmental assessment must be conducted.  If any responses to the questionnaire are 
“yes”, an environmental assessment must be conduc ted. 
 
If an environmental assessment is warranted, refer to Sections 61. 6(a) through 61. 6(e).

62SBL.9  Prior use/ historical sources inquiry ( 02/16/23 ) 
The consultant is to determine whether any past uses of the subject property indicate the 
potential for contamination to be present due to releases of hazardous materials or petroleum.   
 
The physical risk consultant must complete a transaction screen questionnaire substantially 
similar to the current ASTM E1528 standard to initially evaluate this risk.  If any responses to the 
questionnaire are “unknown”, the consultant must explain why th is data gap is not a problem or 
an environmental assessment must be conducted.  If any responses to the questionnaire are 
“yes”, an environmental assessment must be conducted.  
 
If an environmental assessment is warranted, refer to Sections 61. 8(a) through 61. 8(c).
