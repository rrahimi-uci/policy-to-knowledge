c. Deficient payment on an interest -only Mortgage without Reserves (10/31/12)  
1. If the deficiency is $100 or less, the Servicer must : 
 
• Reduce the amount credited to interest by the deficiency  
 
• Reflect the DDLPI as current  
 
• Notify the Borrower of the deficiency  
 
• Collect the deficiency from the Borrower before applying any further payment except to a 
suspense account  
 
2. If the deficiency is greater than $100, the Servicer must : 
 
• Credit the entire payment to a suspense account until the Servicer receives the full 
payment  
 
• Ensure that the DDLPI remains the same as before the deficient payment was recorded  
 
• Inform the Borrower that the deficiency constitutes a Delinquency and must be paid 
immediately  
 
• Collect the deficiency from the Borrower before applying any further payment except to a 
suspense account  
  
d. Delinquency (10/31/12)  
Receipt of consecutive deficient payments constitutes a Delinquency, and the Servicer must 
credit such a payment to the Reserve, if applicable, or a suspense account.
