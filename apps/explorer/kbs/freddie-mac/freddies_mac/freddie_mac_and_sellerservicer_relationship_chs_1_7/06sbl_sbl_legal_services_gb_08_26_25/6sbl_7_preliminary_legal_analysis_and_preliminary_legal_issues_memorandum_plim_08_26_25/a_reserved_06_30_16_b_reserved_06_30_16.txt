provisions that materially change management or control of the Borrower.  
 
A preliminary legal issues memorandum (PLIM) detailing the transaction legal issues is only 
required to be delivered to Freddie Mac (i) for Transfers of Ownership, (ii) certain other 
Special Servicing Requests as specified in the applicable provisions of t his Guide, or (iii) when 
otherwise requested by Freddie Mac.  For Transfers of Ownership or other Special Servicing 
Requests, the PLIM must be prepared using the most current applicable version of the
