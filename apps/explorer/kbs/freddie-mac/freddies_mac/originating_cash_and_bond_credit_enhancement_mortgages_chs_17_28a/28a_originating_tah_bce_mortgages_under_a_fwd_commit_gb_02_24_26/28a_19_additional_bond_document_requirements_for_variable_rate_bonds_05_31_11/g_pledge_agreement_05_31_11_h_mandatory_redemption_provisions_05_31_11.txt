g. Pledge agreement (05/31/11)  
 
At issuance of the variable -rate bonds (for a Funded Forward Commitment) or at Conversion 
(for an Unfunded Forward Commitment), the Borrower must grant Freddie Mac a security 
interest in bonds purchased in whole or in part with amounts paid by Freddie Mac under the 
bond credit enhancement agreement or with withdrawals that Freddie Mac permits to be 
made from the principal reserve fund, to secure the Borrower’s payment of the 
reimbursements, replenishments and liquidity use fees payable under the reimburseme nt and 
security agreement.
