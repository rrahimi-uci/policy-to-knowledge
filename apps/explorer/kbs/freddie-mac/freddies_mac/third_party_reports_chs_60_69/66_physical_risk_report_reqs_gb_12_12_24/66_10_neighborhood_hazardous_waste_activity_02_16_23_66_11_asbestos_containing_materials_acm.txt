Guide Chapter 66 – Conventional/TAH 
Physical Risk  Report  Requirements  
 Storage tanks ( 02/16/23) 
Based on a property inspection and input sought from knowledgeable sources, the physical risk 
consultant must complete a transaction screen questionnaire substantially similar to the current ASTM E1528 standard to initially evaluate this storage tank risk.   If any responses to the questionnaire are 
“yes” or “unknown,” then further analysis must be completed by a consultant qualified in accordance with the requirements of Section 61.17. 
 
If further analysis is warranted, refer to Sections 61. 6(a)-(e). 
 
 Polychlorinated biphenyls (PCBs) ( 02/16/23 ) 
Based on a property inspection and input sought from knowledgeable sources, the physical risk consultant must complete a transaction screen questionnaire substantially similar to the current ASTM 
E1528 standard to initially evaluate this PCB risk.  If any responses to the questionnaire are “yes” or 
“unknown”, then further analysis must be completed by a consultant qualified in accordance with the requirements of Section 61.17. 
 
If further analysis is warranted, refer to Sections 61. 7(a)-(e). 
 
 Prior use/historical sources inquiry ( 02/16/23 ) 
The consultant must determine whether any past uses of the Property indicate the potential for contamination to be present due to releases of hazardous materials or petroleum .  
 Based on a property inspection and input sought from knowledgeable sources, the physical risk 
consultant must complete a transaction screen questionnaire substantially similar to the current ASTM 
E1528 standard to initially evaluate any risk associated wit h the prior use of the Property.  If any 
responses to the questionnaire are “yes” or “unknown, then further analysis must be completed by a consultant qualified in accordance with the requirements of Section 61.17.  
If further analysis is warranted, refer to Sections 61. 8(a)-(c). 
 
 Neighborhood hazardous waste activity ( 02/16/23) 
Refer to Sections 61. 9(a)-(b). 
 
 Asbestos- containing materials (ACM)  (02/16/23)  
For a physical risk consultant with the requisite asbestos training identified in Section 66.18(b), refer 
to Sections 61. 10(a) -(f).  If the physical risk consultant does not have the asbestos training identified 
in Section 66.18(b), then refer to Sections 62SBL. 11(a) -(d). 
 Lead -based paint (02/16/23) 
Refer to Sections 61. 11(a) -(f).
