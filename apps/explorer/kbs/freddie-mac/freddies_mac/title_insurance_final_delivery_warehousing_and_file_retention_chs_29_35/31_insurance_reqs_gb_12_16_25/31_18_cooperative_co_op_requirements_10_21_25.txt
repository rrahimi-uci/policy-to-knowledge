b. Deductibles and self -insured retention (SIR) ( 10/21/25) 
Freddie Mac allows  the following maximum deductible or SIR, or combined deductible and 
SIR for Professional Liability : 
 
• $50,000 for policies insuring a single property  
 
• $250,000 for blanket policies covering multiple properties  
 
Policies that include aggregate deductibles or SIRs are acceptable if the Seller/Servicer 
confirms  the aggregate deductible amount is fully funded and held :  
 
• By the Borrower in a segregated bank account,  
 
• By the Seller/Servicer in a tax and insurance escrow, or  
 
• By a third party on behalf of the Borrower  
 
For existing Mortgages, if the Borrower is unable to obtain a policy with the maximum 
deductibles allowed within the sections of this chapter, the Servicer may approve expanded 
deductibles per Section 31.5 (b). 
  
31.18  Cooperative (Co -op) requirements (10/21/25) 
a. Fidelity bond/crime insurance coverage ( 10/21/25) 
The Seller/Servicer must ensure that each Co -op Borrower maintains fidelity bond/crime 
insurance coverage for the Co -op's employees, officers and board members.  The minimum 
coverage required includes  three  months of scheduled Cooperative Maintenance Fees with a 
maximum deductible of $25,000. 
 
b. Co-op directors’ and officers’ liability insurance ( 10/21/25) 
The Seller/Servicer must ensure that each Co -op maintains directors’ and officers’ liability 
insurance as follows:  
 
• Minimum coverage of $1 million per  claim or per  occurrence  
 
• Maximum deductible of $ 50,000
