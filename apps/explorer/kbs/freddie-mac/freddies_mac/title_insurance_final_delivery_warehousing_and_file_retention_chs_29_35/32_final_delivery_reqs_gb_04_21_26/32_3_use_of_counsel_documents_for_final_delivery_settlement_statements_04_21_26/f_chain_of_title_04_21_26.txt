Guide Chapter 32 – Final Delivery Requirements  
 
Regardless of the method used by the Seller's counsel to obtain the Loan Documents, the 
Seller will be responsible for making the absolute warranty set forth in Section 5.2(c).  
 
c. Settlement statements  and related documentation  (12/16/25) 
The Final Delivery Package for the Borrower must include  the following, which must comply 
with the most current version of Freddie Mac’s “Closing Documentation Guidelines ” posted 
with the Loan Documents : 
 
• A copy of the final executed settlement statement for the Borrower;   
 
• A copy of any  final executed  Property s eller-side settlement statements pertaining to the 
acquisition of the Property;  
 
• A copy of any closing statements and escrow instructions letters provided by Seller or 
Seller’s counsel to the party /parties  conducting the Funding Functions and Escrow 
Functions  for the transaction; and  
 
• A receipts and disbursements ledger for the transaction (for the Mortgage and, if 
applicable, the acquisition of the Property (or ownership interests)) (or other written 
evidence from the Title Company) together with evidence of all corresponding wires or 
checks received or sent with respect to the Mortgage or any acquisition.  
 
d. Seller’s Certification of Outstanding Items  (10/01/25 ) 
All items required to be reviewed and approved by Freddie Mac prior to loan origination or 
prior to loan purchase as noted in the Purchase Contract  must be listed in the Seller’s 
Certification of Outstanding Items, and to the extent approval from Freddie Mac has not been 
received by Seller for any such items at the time the Certification is executed, Seller must 
identify such items on the Certificati on. 
 
e. Written notices  (08/15/24)  
Copies of all written notices that Seller/Servicer’s legal counsel has determined must be 
and/or are generally advisable to be provided to third parties under any documents, 
instruments or agreement, recorded or unrecorded, affecting the Property  in connection with 
the origination of the Mortgage and/or the subsequent assignment of the Mortgage to Freddie 
Mac, must be included with the Final Delivery Package.  This includ es for the purpose of 
entitling the Seller and/or Freddie Mac, each in its capacity as a mortgagee of the Property, to 
any legal rights under such documents, instruments or agreements . 
 
f. Chain of title ( 04/21/26) 
An informational report from the Title Company including copies of all documents evidencing 
the chain of title for the Property for the 36-month period preceding Mortgage origination  (or 
alternatively, the date of such report as specified and subject to the conditions in the Seller’s 
Counsel’s Certification ) must be included with the Final Delivery Package .  This must include  
copies of any deeds transferring all or any portion of the Property , copies of any tax affidavits 
pertaining to all or any portion of the Property,  and copies of any Security Instruments 
encumbering all or any portion of the Property, for such 36-month period.
