Response 
We acknowledge the relatively large 
number of commenters raising the 
possibility that the Department codify a limited data set option in this regulation. Because many of these comments were submitted in response to our proposal to incorporate the same de-identification standard proposed here into § 2.52 (Scientific research), our response to the comments on limited data sets and similar comments related to research are addressed together, below.
