**Section Path:** Justification for Strengthening HIPAA Security Standards
**Summary:** This section argues for the necessity of revising the Security Rule, citing the changing healthcare environment, increasing cybersecurity threats, and inconsistent compliance among regulated entities. It outlines the need for robust standards to ensure the protection of ePHI.
