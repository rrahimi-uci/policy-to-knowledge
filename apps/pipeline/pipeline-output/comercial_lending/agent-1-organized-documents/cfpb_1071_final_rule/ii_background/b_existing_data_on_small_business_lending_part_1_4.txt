B. Existing Data on Small Business Lending  
While small businesses are a critical part of the U.S. economy and require financial 
support , it is still true —as i t was in 2017 when the CFPB  published its White Paper on small 
business lending —that it is not possible with current data to confidently answer basic questions 
regarding the state of small business lending. This limitation is especially the case with regar d to 
the ethnicity, race, and sex of small business owners, applications as opposed to originations, and 
for small business financing products that are not currently reported in Call Report data.60 
Data on small business lending are fragmented, incomplete,  and not standardized, making 
it difficult to conduct meaningful comparisons across products and over time. Against this 
background, it is not hard to see why Congress believed that the collection of small business 
application data would serve  to identify business and community development needs and
