iv. Other Fair Lending Laws  
Several other Federal statutes seek to promote fair lending. The CRA affirmatively 
encourages institutions to help to meet the credit needs of the entire community served by each 
institution covered by the statute, and CRA ratings take into account lending discrimination by those institutions.
193 (See part II.F.2.i below for additional discussion of the CRA.) The 
Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 prohibits discrimination against persons with disabilities in the provision of goods and services, including credit services.
194 Sections 1981195 
and 1982196 of the Federal Civil Rights Acts are broad anti-discrimination laws that have been 
applied to many aspects of credit  transactions.197
