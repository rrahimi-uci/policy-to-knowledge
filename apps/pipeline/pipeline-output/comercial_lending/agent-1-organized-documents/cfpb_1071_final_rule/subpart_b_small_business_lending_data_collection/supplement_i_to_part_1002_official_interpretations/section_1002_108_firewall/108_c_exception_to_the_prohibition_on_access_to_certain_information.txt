employee or officer who knows that an applicant is a minority -owned business , women -owned 
business , or LGBTQI+ -owned business,  or who knows the ethnicity, race, or sex of any of the

making a determination concerning the applicant ’s covered application . Thus, an employee or 
officer who knows, for example, that an applicant is a minority -owned business due to a social

employee or officer that is involved in making a determination concerning  a covered application  
may see, consider, refer to, or use data collected to satisfy aspects of §  1002.107 other than 
§ 1002.107(a)(18) or (19) , such as gross annual revenue, number of workers, and time in 
business.
