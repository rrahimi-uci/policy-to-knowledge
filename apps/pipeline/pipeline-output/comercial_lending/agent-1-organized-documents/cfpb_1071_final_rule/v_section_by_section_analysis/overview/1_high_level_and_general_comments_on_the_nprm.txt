institutions to begin collecting protected applicants ’ demographic in formation beginning 12

D. High -Level and General Comments  
1. High-Level and General Comments on the NPRM  
High -level and general comments received on the NPRM are discussed here, followed by 
a discussion of comments specifically addressing implementation issues and comments regarding 
section 1071’s overlap with other data reporting regimes. Comments received on specific aspects 
of the Bureau ’s proposed rule are discussed in the section -by-section analyses that follow in this 
part V. Comments regarding the privacy analysis are addressed in part VIII below, and regarding 
the Bureau ’s analysis of impacts in parts IX  through XI.
