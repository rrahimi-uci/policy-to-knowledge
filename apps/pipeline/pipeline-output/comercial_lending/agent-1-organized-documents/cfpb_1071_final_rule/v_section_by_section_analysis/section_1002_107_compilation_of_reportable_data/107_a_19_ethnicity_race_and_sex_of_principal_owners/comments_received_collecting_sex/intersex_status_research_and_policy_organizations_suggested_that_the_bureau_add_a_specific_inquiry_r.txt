Intersex status . Research and policy organizations suggested that the Bureau add a 
specific inquiry regarding variations of sex characteristics in the final rule or in the future, to 
identify intersex business owners and their experiences. They emphasized that people with 
variations in sex characteristics may comprise as much as 1.7  percent of the population. And, 
although little population -based data exists, according to the commenter, intersex people face 
documented social and health disparities. This in turn, they said, could affect their economic 
opportunities. Moreover, according t o the commenter, the increased visibility of intersex
