STRATEGIC PLANNING  
Despite the continued decline of the local fishing industry and the increase of local financial service providers, the 
bank’s strategic plan does not adequately address regional economic conditions or local competition. Therefore, the plan may not provide the Board or management with adequate information to assess business opportunities or to adjust strategies and practices in light of changing conditions. The Board should direct correction of the deficiencies in the strategic plan and ensure supporting data is current and comprehensive.
