3. The Closing Package  
 Types of Loan Closing  Packages:  
i. Regular closing package submitted by either non -Priority CDCs or Priority 
CDCs who are not using a Designated Attorney;  and 
ii. Expedited closing package submitted by a Priority CDC using a Designated 
Attorney under the expedited closing  process.  
 The Closing  Package:  
i. CDCs and SBA must use  SBA Form 2286 , “504 Debenture Closing 
Checklist,”  (Checklist ) for all 504 debenture closings. The Checklist lists the 
documents SBA requires to determine whether the debenture can be sold to 
fund the loan. It is not intended to include all the items the CDC will need to 
properly close the  loan.  
ii. SBA requires that the CDC  submit to SBA counsel for  review  a completed 
SBA Form 2286  along with the required items on the Checklist . In rare 
circumstances if an additional document is necessary, the CDC may submit it 
along with an explanation of the  significance.  
 Mandatory  Forms:  
i. Documents on the Checklist that have an SBA form  number  
ii. Opinion of CDC Counsel;  and 
iii. The SBA -approved environmental indemnification  agreement.  
CDCs may use thei r own forms for the lien instruments on Project Property and

approved by SBA counsel prior to  submission . The lien instruments on real estate 
must contain a due -on-sale clause.
