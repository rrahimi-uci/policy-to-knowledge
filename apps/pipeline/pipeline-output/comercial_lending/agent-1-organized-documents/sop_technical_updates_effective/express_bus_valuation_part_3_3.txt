3. Reconsideration of Declined Non-Delegated 7(a)  Small  Loan Applications  
(13 CFR § 120.193 ): 
 If the Lender believes the reason(s) for decline have been overcome, a request for 
reconsideration may be submitted along with a detailed written explanation of 
how the Applicant has overcome the reason(s) for decline. Lender must submit a 
request for reconsideration to the LGPC within 6 months of the date of decline.  
 If a request for re consideration is declined by the LGPC, a second reconsideration  
may be requested from the D/FA  or designee , whose decision is final. The request  
to the D/FA or designee must be submitted to the LGPC and must include a copy 
of the Center’s decline letter an d include additional information that specifically 
addresses the reason(s) identified for decline and how the Applicant has overcome 
those reason(s). The LGPC will forward the request to the D/FA or designee for a 
final decision.
