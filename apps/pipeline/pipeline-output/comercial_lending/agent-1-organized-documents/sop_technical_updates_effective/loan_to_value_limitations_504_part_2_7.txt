iii. Transcripts. The CDC must obtain a copy of the current transcript  of account, 
or equivalent, for the Qualified Debt being refinanced and submit and/or 
retain it as required by SBA Form 1244. See Exhibit 19 of SBA Form 1244. 
The CDC must determine whether the loan to be refinanced involves a 
creditor that is in a positio n to sustain a loss causing a shift to SBA of all or 
part of a potential loss from an existing debt and  document the justification 
for its determination in the credit memorandum. It is prohibited for a 504 
Loan to be used to pay any creditor in a position to sustain a loss causing a 
shift to SBA of all or part of a potential loss from an existing debt under 13 
CFR § 120.884(b) .
