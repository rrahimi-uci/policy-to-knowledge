If the lender has not initiated the IDR process by the 15-day deadline or complied with the demand, Fannie Mae
shall have the option of either initiating the IDR process within 6 months of the end of the management
escalation period or pursuing other remedies.
For additional information about the details of the IDR process, see  Appeal and Independent Dispute Resolution
Processes  posted on Fannie Mae’s website.
