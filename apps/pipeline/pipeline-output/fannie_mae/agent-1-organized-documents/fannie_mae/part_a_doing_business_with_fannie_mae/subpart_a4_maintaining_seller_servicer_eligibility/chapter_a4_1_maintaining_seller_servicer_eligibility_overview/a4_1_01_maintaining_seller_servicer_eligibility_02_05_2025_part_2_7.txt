If a seller does not meet the minimum loan sale requirement below, the seller's approval to sell loans to Fannie
Mae will be terminated and access to all technology that is licensed only to approved sellers will be terminate
within 60 days. If a servicer does not meet the minimum loan servicing requirement below, the servicer's
approval to service loans for Fannie Mae will be terminated and access to all technology that is licensed only to
approved servicers will be terminated. Once terminated as a seller or a servicer, the entity must go through the
seller/servicer application process  to become an approved seller or servicer again and be eligible to sell or
service loans. Fannie Mae will review the application documentation and determine whether the seller or
servicer meets the then current eligibility requirements. Contact the Onboarding Team (see E-1-02, List of
Contacts ) for additional information.
Note:  Sellers/servicers are not required to purchase or own Fannie Mae stock as a condition of
eligibility (as stated in the Mortgage Selling and Servicing Contract).
