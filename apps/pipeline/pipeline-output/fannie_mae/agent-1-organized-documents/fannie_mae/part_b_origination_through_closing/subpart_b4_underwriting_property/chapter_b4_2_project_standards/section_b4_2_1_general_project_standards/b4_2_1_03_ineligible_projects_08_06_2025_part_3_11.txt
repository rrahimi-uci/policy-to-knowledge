Projects that Operate as Hotels or Motels
A project may not be operated or managed as a hotel, motel, or similar commercial entity as evidenced by
meeting one or more of the following criteria:
The HOA is licensed as a hotel, motel, resort, or hospitality entity.
The HOA or project’s legal documents restrict owners’ ability to occupy the unit during any part of the
year.
The HOA or project’s legal documents require owners to make their unit available for rental pooling (daily
or otherwise).
The HOA or the project’s legal documents require unit owners to share proﬁts from the rental of units
with the HOA, management company, or resort, or hotel rental company.
