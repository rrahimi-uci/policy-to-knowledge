Loan-Level Price Adjustments
An LLPA applies to all mortgages secured by manufactured homes that do not meet the MH Advantage
requirements, whether delivered to Fannie Mae for whole loan purchase or MBS issuance. These LLPAs are in
addition to any other price adjustments that are otherwise applicable to the particular transaction. For the
current LLPAs, see the Loan-Level Price Adjustment (LLPA) Matrix  .
Mortgage Insurance
For mortgage insurance coverage requirements, see B7-1-02, Mortgage Insurance Coverage Requirements .
