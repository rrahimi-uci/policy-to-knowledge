Publication of Legal Documents
Legal Documents for Government Mortgages
Publication of Legal Documents
Fannie Mae publishes Legal Documents   on its website. These legal documents include security instruments,
notes, riders and addenda, and special-purpose documents that should be used in connection with regularly
amortizing, conventional, residential mortgage loans sold to Fannie Mae. Many of these forms are published
jointly by Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac and are referred to as Uniform Instruments.
Each legal document published on Fannie Mae's website is accompanied by a Summary or Instructions
