B8-7-01, Mortgage Electronic Registration Systems (MERS), Inc.
(05/01/2024)
Introduction
The MERS system is an electronic system that assists in the tracking of loans, servicing rights, and security
interests. To initiate the electronic tracking, the seller/servicer assigns a special MERS Mortgage Identiﬁcation
Number (MIN) to the loan and registers it in MERS. This topic contains information about MERS, including:

Naming MERS as the Nominee for the Beneﬁciary in the Security Instrument
