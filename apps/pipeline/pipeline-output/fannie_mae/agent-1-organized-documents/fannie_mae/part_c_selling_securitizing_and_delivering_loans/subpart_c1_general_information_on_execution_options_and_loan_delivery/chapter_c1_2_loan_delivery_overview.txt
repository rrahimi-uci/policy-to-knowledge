Chapter C1-2, Loan Delivery Overview
Introduction
Many of the “good delivery” requirements for delivering loans in fulﬁllment of a whole loan commitment diﬀer
from those associated with a commitment to pool loans into an MBS. This chapter provides information about
the delivery requirements that these executions have in common.
