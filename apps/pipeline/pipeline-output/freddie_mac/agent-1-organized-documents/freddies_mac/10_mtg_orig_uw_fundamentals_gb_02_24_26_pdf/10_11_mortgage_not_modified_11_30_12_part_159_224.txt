3. Timing of the Forward Commitment Property Inspection  
 
When submitted as part of the Forward Commitment full underwriting package, the 
Forward Commitment Property Inspection requirements must have been completed 
within 90 days of Freddie Mac’s receipt of the Forward Commitment underwriting 
package.  If not, a new inspection must be performed; no recertification will be allowed.
