The property receives a cash payment from the contract administrator, HUD or the public housing 
agency based on the number of qualifying tenants living in qualifying units.  The Rental Assistance 
Contract remains with the property and does not follow tenan ts to other properties.   
 
Examples of Rental Assistance Contracts include: U.S. Department of Housing and Urban 
Development ( HUD ) Section 8 PBRA or PBV Contracts, NYC 15/15 Project -Based Rental 
Assistance Contracts, or Long Term Subsidy Contracts issued by the District of Columbia Housing 
Authority.  
 
The nature of the Rental Assistance Contract will determine the applicable underwriting 
requirements.  Early in the underwriting process, the Optigo Lender  must inform Freddie Mac of 
the type of Rental Assistance Contract  on the Property.  If the level of rental assistance benefiting 
the Property via a Rental Assistance Contract  is not material to the Property’s income ( less than or 
equal to  20 percent of EGI), Freddie Mac may not underwrite the Mortgage using Rental 
Assistance Cont ract criteria.  
 
If the Property benefits from more than one type of Rental Assistance Contract , Freddie Mac 
requires the Mortgage to meet the underwriting requirements of each Rental Assistance Contract .  
Where underwriting requirements overlap, the Mortgage must be underwritten to comply with the 
most restrictive  rental assistance  standard.  
 
a. Rental Assistance Contract ; definitions ( 08/26/25) 
A Rental Assistance Contract  is considered a Long Term  Rental Assistance Contract  if: 
 
• The term of the Mortgage is less than or equal to 20 years and the term of the Rental 
Assistance Contract  is greater than or equal to the term of the Mortgage  
 
• The term of the Mortgage is greater than 20 years and the term of the Rental Assistance 
Contract  is greater than or equal to 20 years  
 
All other Rental Assistance Contracts  are considered Short Term Rental Assistance 
Contracts . 
 
Other definitions pertinent to  Rental Assistance Contracts  are as follows:  
 
• Above Market, which refers to Rental Assistance Contract  rents that are higher than the 
achievable market rent, also referred to as “ Market  Overhang”  
 
• At or Below Market, which refers to Rental Assistance Contract  rents that are equal to or 
lower than the achievable market rent  
 
b. Rental Assistance Contract income and expense requirements  (08/26/25) 
Freddie Mac underwrites to the lower of market rents, Rental Assistance Contract  rents, or  
restricted  rents, where applicable , and with consideration given to whether there is a Long 
Term Rental Assistance Contract  or Short Term Rental Assistance Contract  in place .
