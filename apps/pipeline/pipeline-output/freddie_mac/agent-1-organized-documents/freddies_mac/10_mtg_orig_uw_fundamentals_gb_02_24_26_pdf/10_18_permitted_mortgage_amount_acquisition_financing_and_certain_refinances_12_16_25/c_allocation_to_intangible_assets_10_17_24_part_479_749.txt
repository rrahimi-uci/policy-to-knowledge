Guide Chapter 46SBL – SBL Collateral, 
Repurchase  and Loss Obligations  
• The sum of each of the following:  
 
o The unpaid principal balance of the Defaulted Mortgage as of the acquisition date or 
the date of acceptance of a deed in lieu of foreclosure  
 
o Accrued interest at the Accounting Net Yield rate from the Due Date of the Last Paid Installment through the day before the Seller/Servicer’s  purchase  of the REO 
Property  
 
o Any expenses reimbursed by Freddie Mac to the Servicer  
 
o Any expenses incurred by Freddie Mac in marketing the REO Property  
 
o The greater  of (a) any prepayment premium that would otherwise be payable by 
Borrower, pursuant to the terms of the Loan Documents, on the date the Property 
became an REO Property, or (b) any hedge breakage costs incurred by Freddie Mac 
related to unwinding its hedge on the Mortgage  
 
• Reduced by each of the following:  
 
o Any sale proceeds  
 
o Other proceeds or refunds remitted to Freddie Mac by or on behalf of the Servicer, except any rental proceeds remitted or due to Freddie Mac  
 
At Freddie Mac's discretion, the SBL REO Price will include an amount equal to any loss, 
damage or expense, including court costs, costs of investigation and reasonable attorney fees, incurred by Freddie Mac in connection with its purchase, ownership and r esale to the 
Seller /Servicer  of Freddie Mac's interest in the REO Property.  In addition, the 
Seller /Servicer  must pay all documentary stamp taxes, recording fees, transfer taxes and 
all other expenses payable in connection with the transfer of the REO Property to the Seller /Servicer , including legal fees.
