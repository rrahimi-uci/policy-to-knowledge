A depository institution that is a subsidiary of a holding company or an affiliate of another 
depository may, on a case -by-case basis, have its eligibility rating affected by the rating of its 
holding company or affiliate.   FDIC regulations may require related depository institutions to 
guarantee the obligations of the troubled depository institutions.   As a result, the related 
depository institution may be subject to a risk of regulatory action even if it meets Freddie 
Mac's minimum rating requirements and  Freddie Mac may deem it to be ineligible.  The 
Servicer should consider this when selecting or monitoring a depository.
