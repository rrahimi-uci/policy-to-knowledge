c. Conflicts of interest/provision of related services (1 2/16/25 ) 
The Utility Efficiency  Consultant may not be affiliated with the Borrower, the Seller/Servicer, a 
buyer or seller of the Property, or engaged in any business that might present a conflict of 
interest.  The Utility Efficiency  Consultant may not be engaged to perform any repair or remedial 
work specified in the Green Assessment . 
 
d. Insurance (1 2/16/25 ) 
The Utility Efficiency  Consultant must provide to the Seller/Servicer a copy of the original 
certificate(s) of insurance that indicates that the Utility Efficiency  Consultant is, at its own 
expense, covered by insurance meeting the requirements of Section 11.5.  
The Seller/Servicer must retain the copy of the original certificate(s) of liability insurance in its 
files. 
 
e. Unacceptable Utility Efficiency  Consultants (1 2/16/25 ) 
The Seller/Servicer must send written notification immediately to the Applicable Freddie Mac 
Multifamily Regional Office  if the Seller/Servicer, for cause, discontinues the use of a Utility 
Efficiency  Consultant who has completed Green Assessment  within the past 12 months for 
Mortgages purchased or credit enhanced by Freddie Mac.  The TAH Seller/Servicer must send 
written notification immediately to the Multifamily TAH Underwriter . 
In addition, Freddie Mac reserves the right to refuse to accept Green Assessment  completed by 
any specific Utility Efficiency  Consultant.  Freddie Mac will maintain, at mf.freddiemac.com , the 
Multifamily Restricted Vendor List .  If a Utility Efficiency  Consultant appears on the Multifamily 
Restricted Vendor List, the Seller/Servicer may not use that Utility Efficiency  Consultant to inspect 
or perform an energy and water audit for a Property until notified otherwise by Freddie Mac.  The 
decision to place a third -party vendor on the Multifamily Restricted Vendor List is solely within 
Freddie Mac’s discretion.  
The Multifamily Restricted Vendor List is made available to Seller/Servicers for the sole purpose 
of ensuring that unacceptable Utility Efficiency  Consultants do not prepare reports for Multifamily 
and will constitute “Confidential Information” as defined in Section 2.8.
