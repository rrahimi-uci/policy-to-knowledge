The Seller must determine, and Freddie Mac must approve, the amount of the Mortgage

f. Additional Mortgage proceeds (05/31/11)  
 
Additional Mortgage proceeds may be available for a Property with a current net operating 
income (NOI) that exceeds the underwritten NOI.  Any additional Mortgage proceeds must be 
approved by Freddie Mac after Freddie Mac’s full re -underwriting.  The addit ional proceeds 
will be re -priced as a first Mortgage, although Freddie Mac’s security for such additional 
proceeds may be in the form of a subordinate Mortgage.
