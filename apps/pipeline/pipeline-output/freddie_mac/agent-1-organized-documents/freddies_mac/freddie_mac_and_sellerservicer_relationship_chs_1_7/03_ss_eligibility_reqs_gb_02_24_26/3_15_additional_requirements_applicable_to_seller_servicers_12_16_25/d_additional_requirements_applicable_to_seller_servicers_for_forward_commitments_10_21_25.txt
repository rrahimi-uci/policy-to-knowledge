d. Additional requirements applicable to Seller/Servicers  for Forward Commitments  
(10/21/25 ) 
  Each Seller/Servicer entering into a Forward Commitment pursuant to Chapter 17A  must have:  
 
• One or more underwriting supervisors with approximately seven to 10 years of experience in 
underwriting multifamily properties with forward commitments and substantial rehabilitations.  
 
• Designated staff with 10 or more years of experience with forward commitments, substantial 
rehabilitations and other construction -related loans for multifamily properties.   
 
• Staff or a relationship with a third party Chief Architect/Engineer with the following 
background:  
 
o At least five years’ experience in construction lending and the origination and 
management of construction loans, and  
 
o A professional background or accredited degree in the field of engineering, architecture 
or construction management, or 10 years of experience and demonstrated knowledge of 
building materials and design, construction processes and documentation, cost analy sis, 
and project scheduling  
 
3.16 Seller/Servicer’s fidelity and errors & omissions insurance coverage  (02/16/23) 
The Seller/Servicer must maintain in effect, at all times and at its expense, fidelity insurance and 
mortgagee’s errors and omissions (E&O) insurance that meets all of the requirements of this Section.
