c. Scope of Servicer and Single Counsel review (06/30/16)  
The Servicer and Single Counsel must ensure that  
 
• They have reviewed the documents in detail  
 
• The documents fully reflect all terms of Freddie Mac's approval and do not otherwise 
change any of the terms of the SBL Mortgage  
 
• All statements set forth in the documents are accurate  
 
• The documents comply with all applicable federal, State and local laws
