21.19  General Servicing policy for Seniors Housing Mortgages ( 04/15/21 ) 
Unless otherwise stated, the Servicing of each Seniors Housing Mortgage must meet the 
requirements of Chapters 36 through 43.  
 
a. Property performance reporting frequency ( 04/15/21 ) 
Unless otherwise specified in the Loan Documents, the Servicer must perform periodic 
assessments of each Seniors Housing Mortgage in accordance with the provisions of 
Chapter 40.
