Guide Chapter 25 – Originating a Targeted 
Affordable Housing Tax -Exempt Loan  
 
 Yield maintenance period  (08/17 /23) 
 
The provisions of Section 27.30(c) are inapplicable to TEL Mortgages.  
 
 Final delivery ; Funding  (08/17 /23) 
The provisions of Chapter 32 apply , with the exception that the Final Delivery Table of Contents refers 
to the Final Delivery Package Table of Contents –Immediate  Funding Tax -Exempt Loan . 
 
 UCC continuation and termination statements ( 08/17/23 ) 
The Seller’s responsibility for filing UCC  (Uniform Commercial Code) continuation and termination 
statements applies only to financing statements under which Freddie Mac is the secured party and 
does not apply to financing statements under which the govern mental lender or the fiscal agent is the 
only secured party.  With respect to any financing statement under which Freddie Mac is the secured 
party, the Seller must not file a termination statement until the obligations of the governmental lender 
under the tax-exempt governmental note have terminated and all obligations of the Borrower under 
the Note and the project loan agreement have been satisfied.  See Section  29.4 and the Final 
Delivery Instructions found at mf.freddiemac.com/lenders/purchase  for further information about UCC 
financing statements.  
 
 General  Servicing policy  for TELs  (08/26/25) 
Unless otherwise stated in the Letter of Commitment or this chapter, the Servicing of each TEL must 
meet the requirements of Chapters  36 and 38 through  43. 
 
 Servicing Spread (08/ 26/25) 
The Servicer’s servicing fee for each Mortgage will be the Servicing Spread set forth in the 
Servicing Spreads Chart  posted on our website and will be stated in the Purchase Contract for 
that Mortgage. If there is a difference between the Servicing Spread set forth in the Servicing 
Spreads Chart and the Servicing Spread set forth in the Purchase Contract , the Purchase 
Contract  will govern . The Servicing Spread is a fee payable under the project loan agreement.  
The Servicer will earn the servicing Spread  when it collects the Borrower’s payments under the 
project note and the project loan agreement.   
 
 Reserved (08/17/23)  
 
 Subordinate Financing, partial release, condemnation or easement (09/28/18)  
Any required submission from the Seller to Freddie Mac regarding a proposed Subordinate 
Financing, partial release, condemnation or easement must include a summary of any applicable 
provisions of the documents associated with the loan transaction.  
 
 Advance notice of prepayment (09/28/18)  
The Servicer must forward a copy of any advance notice of prepayment to the fiscal agent at the 
same time that the Seller forwards that notice to Multifamily Loan Accounting .
