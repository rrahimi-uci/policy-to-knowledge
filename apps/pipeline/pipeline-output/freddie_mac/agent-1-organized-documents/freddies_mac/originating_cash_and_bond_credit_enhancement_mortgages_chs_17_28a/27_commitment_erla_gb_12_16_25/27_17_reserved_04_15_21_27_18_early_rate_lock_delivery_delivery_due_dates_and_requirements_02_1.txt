27.16  Rate Lock and Mortgage Terms Confirmation Sheet  (04/15/21)  
a. Confirmation of Rate Lock Terms  (04/15/21)  
After Rate Lock, Freddie Mac will deliver to the Seller a completed Confirmation Sheet.  
 
No later than the Business Day  immediately following the Seller’s receipt of the Confirmation 
Sheet, the Seller must execute and upload to DMS the Confirmation Sheet to indicate its 
acceptance of the Rate Lock terms.  
 
b. Failure to Return Confirmation Sheet  (04/15/21)  
If the Seller fails to return the executed Confirmation Sheet to Freddie Mac within the required 
time, Freddie Mac may, in its discretion, treat such failure as a Nondelivery .
