c. Timing of property inspections  and lease audits  (06/13/24 ) 
The inspection and lease audit requirements of Section 8SBL.15(b) must have been completed 
within 90 days of Freddie Mac’s receipt of the full underwriting package.  
 
If the Seller inspection and lease audit are not completed within 90 days of Freddie Mac’s 
receipt of the underwriting package, a new inspection and lease audit must be performed; no 
recertification will be allowed.
