c. Requesting an adjustment of the Annual Financial due date (04/30/19)  
The Servicer may contact Freddie Mac Multifamily Asset Management, Asset Performance and 
Compliance  via PRS to request an adjustment of the Annual Financial due date if:  
 
• The Borrower's fiscal year end would prevent the Servicer from submitting the Annual 
Financial by June 30 or other date required by Freddie Mac.  
 
• The Mortgage was for the acquisition of the Property, and the purchase occurred so late in 
the preceding calendar year that the number of months of data available for review would 
prevent the Servicer from making a meaningful evaluation.  
 
d. Reporting to senior Mortgage servicers (03/03/17)  
If the Servicer is reporting Annual Financial or Quarterly Financial information to Freddie Mac 
with respect to a Supplemental Mortgage related to one or more securitized senior lien 
Mortgages, the Servicer is authorized to provide that same information to  the Servicer(s) of the 
securitized senior Mortgage(s).
