e. Remittance of fees (06/29/18 ) 
Within five Business Days after the conditionally permitted Transfer of Ownership occurs, the 
Servicer must remit by wire transfer to Freddie Mac any fees required pursuant to the terms of 
the pre -authorization provisions of the Loan Documents, in accordan ce with the requirements of 
Section 41.9.
