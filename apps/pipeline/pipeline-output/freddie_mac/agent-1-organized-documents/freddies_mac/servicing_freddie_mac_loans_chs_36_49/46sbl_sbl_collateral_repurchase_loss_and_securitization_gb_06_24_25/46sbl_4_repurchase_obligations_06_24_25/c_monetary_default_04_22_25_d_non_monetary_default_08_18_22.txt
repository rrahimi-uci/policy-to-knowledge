d. Non-monetary default  (08/18/22 ) 
The Seller /Servicer is also subject to an SBL Repurchase Obligation if an SBL Borrower is 
in default under the applicable SBL Mortgage for any of the following non- monetary “Events 
of Default” described in the Loan Agreement for that SBL Mortgage (“Non- Monetary 
Defaults”):  
 
• Section 9.01(d) or Section 8.01(b) (fraud, material misrepresentation or material omission)  
 
• Section 9.01(f) or Section 8.01(g) (transfers that violate the provisions of Article VII of the Loan Agreement, including liens on the Mortgaged Property)  
 
• Section 9.01(g) or Section 8.01(h) (forfeiture proceeding)  
 
• Section 9.01(j and n) or Section 8.01(i and j) (uncured defaults(s) under other lien(s) on the Mortgaged Property)  
 
• Section 9.01(k) or Section 8.01 (l and m) (bankruptcy, insolvency and related matters)  
 
• Section 9.01(p) or Section 8.01(n) (Guarantor bankruptcy)
