specified in Section 1.31 of Exhibit 1  for a transaction under the Cash Forward Commitment

Housing Bond Credit Enhancement Forward Commitment Program.  For additional 
information regarding the construction completion documents, see Section 55.2.

third-party reports and the construction reports set forth in Section 5.4.
