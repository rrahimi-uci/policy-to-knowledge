Guide Chapter 31 – Insurance Requirements  
a. Wind/ Hail coverage (04/30/19)  
Wind/ Hail coverage must meet the requirements identified in Sections 31.5 and 31.6, with the 
exception of deductibles.  
 
b. Wind/ Hail deductibles ( 12/16/25) 
For Specific Insurance Limit s: 
 
• When expressed as a dollar amount:  
 
IV of the Property  Maximum deductible  
< $10 million  $50,000  
≥ $10 million  $100,000 
 
• When expressed as a percentage, five percent of the subject property’s TIV with a stated 
minimum not to exceed $100,000  
 
For Blanket Insurance Limit s: 
 
• When expressed as a dollar amount, $250,000  
 
• When expressed as a percentage, five percent of the subject property’s TIV, with a stated 
minimum not to exceed $250,000  
 
The maximum deductible for Wind/Hail business income/rental value coverage is:  
 
• When expressed as number of days, 15 days  
 
• When expressed as a dollar amount, $100,000  
 
c. Named Storm coverage (0 7/15/25) 
For all properties located in Tier 1 Windstorm Risk counties, as defined by the insurer, 
Named Storm coverage for stand -alone policies must meet the requirements in Sections 31.5 
and 31.6, with the exception of deductibles.  
 
If the N amed Storm coverage does not include flooding due to storm surge, then the 
Borrower is to maintain flood insurance for any buildings located in FEMA 500 -year flood 
zones equal to or exceeding the maximum coverage available through NFIP policies or their 
equivalent.  
 
If Named Storm coverage is provided as part of a Blanket Insurance Limit, the Seller/Servicer 
must determine, to its satisfaction, that the blanket Named Storm limits are adequate for the 
Property and any concentrations of Insurable Value associated with other properties covered  
per Section 31.5(c).

d. Named Storm deductibles ( 12/16/25) 
For Specific Insurance Limit s: 
 
• When expressed as a dollar amount:  
  
IV of the Property  Maximum deductible  
< $10 million  $50,000  
≥ $10 million  $100,000 
 
• When expressed as a percentage, 7.5 percent of the subject property’s TIV, with a stated 
minimum not to exceed $100,000  
 
For Blanket Insurance Limit s: 
 
• When expressed as a dollar amount, $250,000  
 
• When expressed as a percentage, 7.5 percent  of the subject property’s TIV, with a stated 
minimum not to exceed $250,000  
 
The maximum deductible for Named Storm business income/rental value coverage is:  
 
• When expressed as number of days, 15 days  
 
• When expressed as a dollar amount, $100,000  
 
e. Windstorm insurance through a State Windpool ( 12/16/25) 
If windstorm coverage is only available from a state -sponsored insurance program  or State 
Windpool , the policy must:  
 
• Be written in an amount no less than 100 percent of the TIV and meet the deductible 
requirements in this Section 31. 7. 
 
• If the policy contains a Coinsurance Clause , it must : 
 
o Include an Agreed Amount Provision  equal to  no less than 100 percent of the IV of 
the improvements , or 
 
o Contain a Coinsurance Clause less than or equal to 80 percent
