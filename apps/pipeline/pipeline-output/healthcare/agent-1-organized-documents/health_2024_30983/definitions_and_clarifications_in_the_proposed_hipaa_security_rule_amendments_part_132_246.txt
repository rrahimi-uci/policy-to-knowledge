**Section Path:** Definitions and Clarifications in the Proposed HIPAA Security Rule Amendments
**Summary:** This section provides a comprehensive list of definitions and clarifications proposed for the Security Rule, including terms related to access, authentication, and security measures. It aims to enhance clarity and understanding of compliance obligations for regulated entities.

Beyond the health care sector, threat 
actors have been able to gain access to networks by compromising user accounts and taking advantage of insufficient network segregation. For example, the 2014 Home Depot breach involved the compromise of a third- party vendor’s username and password to enter Home Depot’s network, which allowed hackers to obtain elevated rights to navigate to self-checkout point- of-sale system.
694The Department is 
concerned about the potential effects of such incidents in health care, where they would jeopardize the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of ePHI.
