Section 164.312(c)(1)—Standard: 
Configuration Management 
The Department believes that the 
failure to configure technical controls appropriately and to establish and maintain secure baselines for relevant electronic information systems and technology assets in its relevant electronic information systems presents an opportunity for cyberattack and compromise of ePHI.
747Accordingly, 
we propose to add a standard for configuration management at proposed 45 CFR 164.312(c)(1). The proposed standard would require a regulated entity to establish and deploy technical controls for securing relevant electronic information systems and technology assets in its relevant electronic information systems, including workstations, in a consistent manner.
