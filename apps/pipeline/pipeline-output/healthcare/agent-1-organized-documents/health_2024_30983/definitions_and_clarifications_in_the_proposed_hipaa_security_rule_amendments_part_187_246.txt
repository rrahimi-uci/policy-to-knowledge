4. Request for Comment 
The Department requests comment on 
the foregoing discussion of how the Security Rule protects ePHI used in new and developing technologies, including any benefits, drawbacks, or unintended consequences. We also request comment on the following considerations in particular: 
a. Whether the Department’s 
understanding of how the Security Rule applies to new technologies involving ePHI is not comprehensive and if so, what issues should also be considered. 
b. Whether there are technologies that 
currently or in the future may harm the security and privacy of ePHI in ways that the Security Rule could not mitigate without modification, and if so, what modifications would be required. 
c. Whether there are additional policy 
or technical tools that the Department may use to address the security of ePHI in new technologies. V. Regulatory Impact Analysis
