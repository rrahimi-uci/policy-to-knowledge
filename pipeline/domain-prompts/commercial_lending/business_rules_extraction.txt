RULE TYPE TAXONOMY (10 Categories)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Classify each rule into exactly ONE of these 10 categories:

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE 10 RULE TYPES (Mutually Exclusive)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. **credit_policy** - Borrower qualification standards and credit approval criteria
   Detection: "minimum DSCR", "maximum leverage", "risk rating", "credit score", "eligible borrower", "underwriting standard"
   Examples:
   - "Minimum DSCR of 1.25x required on all CRE term loans at origination"
   - "Global cash flow analysis required for all borrowers with aggregate exposure > $1MM"
   - "Borrowers with Risk Grade 7 or worse are ineligible for new credit extensions"

2. **collateral** - Collateral eligibility, valuation, and LTV/LTC requirements
   Detection: "LTV", "LTC", "appraisal", "collateral", "security", "lien position", "environmental"
   Examples:
   - "Maximum LTV 75% for stabilized income-producing commercial real estate"
   - "Appraisal by MAI-certified appraiser required for CRE collateral > $500,000"
   - "Phase I Environmental Site Assessment required for all CRE-secured loans > $250,000"

3. **covenant** - Financial maintenance covenants and reporting covenant requirements
   Detection: "covenant", "maintenance test", "compliance certificate", "DSCR test", "leverage covenant", "liquidity covenant"
   Examples:
   - "Minimum DSCR covenant of 1.20x tested quarterly on trailing 12-month basis"
   - "Maximum leverage ratio (Total Debt / EBITDA) of 4.0x tested semi-annually"
   - "Compliance certificate required within 60 days of fiscal year-end"

4. **regulatory** - CRA, Reg B, Reg O, BSA, HMDA, Basel III, and other regulatory requirements
   Detection: "CRA", "Reg B", "Reg O", "HMDA", "Basel", "capital requirement", "fair lending", "ECOA"
   Examples:
   - "Reg O aggregate limit for all insider loans not to exceed 15% of unimpaired capital"
   - "HMDA reportable loans must be submitted on LAR by March 1 of following year"
   - "CRA large bank exam includes lending test, investment test, and service test"

5. **documentation** - Required loan documents, legal opinions, and due diligence
   Detection: "required document", "loan agreement", "security agreement", "opinion letter", "due diligence", "closing checklist"
   Examples:
   - "UCC-1 financing statement must be filed within 30 days of loan closing"
   - "Corporate resolution authorizing borrowing required for all entity borrowers"
   - "Title insurance policy required for all CRE-secured loans > $1,000,000"

6. **underwriting** - Financial analysis standards, DSCR/cash flow methodology, and stress testing
   Detection: "global cash flow", "EBITDA", "NOI", "stress test", "sensitivity analysis", "spread analysis"
   Examples:
   - "NOI calculated as gross rental income minus vacancy (5% minimum) and operating expenses"
   - "Interest rate stress test required at 200bps above origination rate for all floating rate loans"
   - "Global cash flow must include all personal and business debt obligations of guarantors"

7. **risk_assessment** - Loan grading, concentration limits, watch list criteria, and portfolio monitoring
   Detection: "risk rating", "loan grade", "watch list", "criticized asset", "concentration limit", "ALLL"
   Examples:
   - "Commercial real estate concentration may not exceed 300% of total risk-based capital"
   - "Loans rated 6 or worse must be reviewed by special assets within 30 days of downgrade"
   - "Annual loan review required for all credits with aggregate exposure ≥ $500,000"

8. **compliance** - BSA/AML, OFAC, beneficial ownership, fair lending, flood insurance requirements
   Detection: "BSA", "AML", "OFAC", "beneficial ownership", "flood insurance", "fair lending", "CIP"
   Examples:
   - "Beneficial ownership certification required for all new legal entity customers"
   - "OFAC screening required at origination and at any material change in borrower profile"
   - "NFIP flood insurance required when collateral is in SFHA and loan > $5,000"

9. **pricing** - Rate floors, risk-based pricing, pricing exceptions, and spread requirements
   Detection: "rate floor", "spread", "pricing exception", "risk-based pricing", "fee", "origination fee"
   Examples:
   - "Minimum spread of 200bps over benchmark rate required for all CRE loans"
   - "Pricing exceptions require one level above originating officer approval"
   - "Origination fee of 1.0% minimum on construction loans; 0.50% on permanent loans"

10. **reporting** - HMDA LAR, Call Report, CRA reporting, credit bureau, and regulatory filing obligations
    Detection: "HMDA", "Call Report", "CRA", "LAR", "regulatory report", "credit bureau", "Y-9C"
    Examples:
    - "HMDA LAR must be submitted to CFPB by March 1 annually for prior year originations"
    - "Charge-offs exceeding $100,000 must be reported on quarterly Call Report Schedule C"
    - "CRA public file must be available and updated within 30 days of any material change"

EXTRACTION QUALITY STANDARDS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Each business rule MUST include:

✓ **rule_id**: Globally unique with batch number
✓ **rule_name**: Human-readable natural language title with specific thresholds — no underscores or CamelCase
✓ **rule_type**: One of 10 types: credit_policy|collateral|covenant|regulatory|documentation|underwriting|risk_assessment|compliance|pricing|reporting
✓ **description**: Complete explanation with specific numeric thresholds
✓ **conditions**: When/where rule applies
✓ **consequences**: What happens if met/violated
✓ **exceptions**: Special cases or N/A
✓ **source_reference**: chunk_path, section_id, start_word_position, end_word_position, source_text
✓ **mandatory**: true/false
✓ **examples**: 2-3 concrete scenarios
✓ **confidence_score**: 0-100
✓ **confidence_breakdown**: extraction_clarity, numeric_precision, context_completeness, source_authority, logical_consistency
✓ **effective_date**: YYYY-MM-DD or null
✓ **expiration_date**: null or YYYY-MM-DD
✓ **superseded_by**: null or rule_id
✓ **jurisdiction**: "federal" | "state:<XX>" | "agency:OCC" | "agency:FDIC" | "agency:FRB" | "agency:CFPB" | "agency:SBA" | "bank_policy"
✓ **risk_level**: "critical" | "high" | "medium" | "low"
✓ **related_rules**: Array of related rule_ids
✓ **enforcement_action**: Violation consequence (e.g., "loan declassification", "regulatory criticism", "civil money penalty")
✓ **applicability_scope**:
  - loan_types: Array (e.g., ["term_loan", "revolving_credit", "construction_loan", "SBA"])
  - borrower_types: Array (e.g., ["commercial_real_estate", "C&I", "small_business", "agricultural"])
  - transaction_types: Array (e.g., ["origination", "renewal", "modification", "annual_review"])
✓ **data_points_required**: Array of data fields needed to evaluate the rule
✓ **audit_frequency**: "at_origination" | "at_renewal" | "monthly" | "quarterly" | "semi_annually" | "annually" | "on_change"
