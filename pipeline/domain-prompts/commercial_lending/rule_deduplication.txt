**5. Consequence Alignment (5 points maximum)**

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DEDUPLICATION PRINCIPLES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. **CONSERVATIVE APPROACH - When In Doubt, Keep Both**
   ❌ DO NOT merge if ANY of these differ:
   - Specific ratios or thresholds (1.25x DSCR vs 1.20x DSCR vs 1.35x DSCR)
   - Loan types (owner-occupied CRE vs investment CRE vs construction)
   - Borrower categories (C&I vs CRE vs small business vs SBA)
   - Regulatory body (OCC vs FDIC vs FRB vs CFPB vs SBA)
   - Dollar thresholds ($500,000 vs $1,000,000 vs $250,000)
   - Transaction types (origination vs renewal vs modification)

2. **IDENTIFY TRUE DUPLICATES**
   ✅ Rules ARE duplicates if ALL of these match:
   - Identical business requirement/constraint
   - Same numeric ratios and thresholds
   - Same regulatory context and loan scope
   - Same consequences for violation
   - Only differ in wording/phrasing
   - Total score ≥ 75 points

   Examples of TRUE duplicates:
   - "Minimum DSCR of 1.25x required on all CRE term loans at origination" vs "CRE term loan origination requires debt service coverage of no less than 1.25 times" (Score: ~95)
   - "Phase I Environmental Site Assessment required for CRE collateral exceeding $250,000" vs "Environmental review (Phase I ESA) mandatory for all commercial real estate loans over $250K" (Score: ~92)

3. **PRIMARY RULE SELECTION PRIORITY**
   Priority 1: Completeness → Priority 2: Specificity → Priority 3: Source Authority → Priority 4: Mandatory Status
