RULE TYPE TAXONOMY (10 Categories)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Classify each rule into exactly ONE of these 10 categories:

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
THE 10 RULE TYPES (Mutually Exclusive)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. **clinical_guideline** - Evidence-based standards of care and treatment protocols
   Detection: "standard of care", "best practice", "clinical pathway", "evidence-based", "protocol"
   Examples:
   - "Sepsis bundle must be initiated within 1 hour of diagnosis"
   - "VTE prophylaxis required for all surgical patients with LOS > 24 hours"
   - "Antibiotic de-escalation reassessment required at 72 hours"

2. **patient_safety** - Rules preventing patient harm, adverse events, and medical errors
   Detection: "never event", "safety goal", "harm prevention", "adverse event", "near miss", "sentinel event"
   Examples:
   - "Universal protocol (time-out) required immediately before every surgical procedure"
   - "Two-patient identifiers required prior to medication administration"
   - "Fall risk assessment using validated tool required within 8 hours of admission"

3. **hipaa_privacy** - PHI protection, access controls, and breach notification requirements
   Detection: "PHI", "protected health information", "minimum necessary", "authorization", "breach notification"
   Examples:
   - "PHI breach affecting ≥500 individuals must be reported to HHS within 60 days"
   - "Business associate agreement required before any PHI disclosure to third party"
   - "Minimum necessary standard applies to all PHI uses and disclosures"

4. **billing_compliance** - Coding accuracy, claim submission rules, and reimbursement requirements
   Detection: "ICD-10", "CPT code", "DRG", "claim submission", "RAC audit", "upcoding", "bundling"
   Examples:
   - "Principal diagnosis must reflect condition responsible for admission after study"
   - "Modifier 25 required when E&M service performed same day as procedure"
   - "Medicare claims must be filed within 12 months of date of service"

5. **documentation** - Medical record requirements, EHR standards, and retention rules
   Detection: "medical record", "documentation requirement", "record retention", "attestation", "co-signature"
   Examples:
   - "History and physical must be completed within 24 hours of inpatient admission"
   - "Operative report must be dictated immediately following surgery"
   - "Medical records must be retained for minimum 10 years from date of last service"

6. **consent_requirement** - Patient consent for treatment, procedures, research, and data use
   Detection: "informed consent", "patient authorization", "consent form", "right to refuse"
   Examples:
   - "Written informed consent required for all surgical procedures prior to sedation"
   - "HIPAA authorization required for disclosure of PHI for marketing purposes"
   - "Research consent must include 8 required elements per 45 CFR 46.116"

7. **credentialing** - Provider licensing, certification, privileging, and reappointment requirements
   Detection: "medical license", "board certification", "clinical privileges", "credentialing", "NPDB query"
   Examples:
   - "NPDB query required for initial credentialing and every 2 years at reappointment"
   - "DEA registration must be current and unrestricted for all prescribers"
   - "Focused professional practice evaluation required for all newly privileged providers"

8. **quality_measure** - Performance metrics, outcome measures, and quality reporting requirements
   Detection: "HEDIS", "core measure", "quality metric", "performance rate", "CAHPS", "readmission"
   Examples:
   - "30-day all-cause readmission rate exceeding CMS threshold triggers HRRP penalty"
   - "Hospital-acquired infection rate must be reported to NHSN monthly"
   - "Patient experience survey response rate minimum 300 or 100% of eligible discharges"

9. **regulatory** - CMS Conditions of Participation, Joint Commission, FDA, and state licensing rules
   Detection: "Condition of Participation", "CoP", "TJC standard", "state licensure", "CMS survey", "deficiency"
   Examples:
   - "Governing body meeting minutes must be maintained for CMS CoP compliance"
   - "Emergency preparedness plan must be reviewed and updated annually"
   - "All dietary services must comply with CMS CoP 42 CFR 482.28"

10. **reporting** - Mandatory reporting obligations to regulatory and public health authorities
    Detection: "mandatory reporting", "adverse event report", "sentinel event", "notifiable disease", "state report"
    Examples:
    - "Sentinel events must be reported to Joint Commission within 5 calendar days"
    - "Communicable diseases on state reportable list must be reported within 24 hours"
    - "Surgical site infections must be reported to NHSN within 30 days of procedure"

CONFIDENCE SCORING RUBRIC
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

For EVERY rule, assess confidence using this 5-factor rubric:

**1. EXTRACTION CLARITY (0-100):**
- 90-100: Explicit "must/shall/required" language with clear thresholds
- 70-89: Clear requirement using descriptive language
- 50-69: Implied requirement inferred from context
- Below 50: Ambiguous or uncertain extraction

**2. NUMERIC PRECISION (0-100):**
- 100: Exact numbers stated ("within 60 days", "≥ 500 individuals", "2-year interval")
- 75: Range provided
- 50: Approximate
- 25: Qualitative only
- 0: No numeric thresholds

**3. CONTEXT COMPLETENESS (0-100):**
- 90-100: All conditions, consequences, and exceptions clearly stated
- 70-89: Main conditions clear, minor gaps
- 50-69: Conditions stated but consequences or exceptions unclear
- Below 50: Incomplete context

**4. SOURCE AUTHORITY (0-100):**
- 100: Direct quote from official regulatory section
- 80: Clear derivation from primary source
- 60: Inferred from multiple related sections
- 40: Derived from examples or implicit requirements

**5. LOGICAL CONSISTENCY (0-100):**
- 100: Rule is internally consistent
- 80: Minor terminology variations but logically sound
- 60: Some ambiguity
- Below 60: Logical gaps or contradictions

**CONFIDENCE_SCORE CALCULATION:**
Overall = (Extraction Clarity × 0.30) + (Numeric Precision × 0.25) + (Context Completeness × 0.20) + (Source Authority × 0.15) + (Logical Consistency × 0.10)

Rules with confidence_score < 70 should include "requires_review": true
