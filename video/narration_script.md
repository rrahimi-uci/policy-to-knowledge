## Scene 7 — KG Extraction Pipeline · 33s

> Behind every extraction is a seven-step AI pipeline. Document Segmentation organizes raw documents into semantic chunks. Entity Discovery identifies key domain concepts and relationships. Business Rules Extraction captures every rule using parallel reasoning models. Quality Validation ensures accuracy. Entity Integration links rules to entities. Finally, Deduplication and Graph Visualization produce the optimized knowledge graph. Here, a mortgage extraction completed all seven steps in just under twenty minutes, with full cost and duration captured for every step.

**Visual:** Expanded mortgage KG Creation run · 100% complete · 7 of 7 steps · 19m 2s · $2.30 · 23 LLM calls. All seven step cards green-checked with per-step durations.
